Plum Cake

Plum cake refers to a wide range of cakes made with either dried fruit or with fresh fruit.

There is a wide range of popular plum cakes and puddings.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 ¼ cups plums, pitted and quartered

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease and flour one 9-inch tube pan.
  2. Separate the eggs. In a small bowl, beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form, and set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream the butter and sugar. Beat in the egg yolks and the lemon zest.
  4. Stir together the flour and baking powder and then blend the flour mixture into the creamed mixture.
  5. Bake in preheated oven until a tester inserted in the center comes out clean, about 40 minutes.
